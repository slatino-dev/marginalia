# PRD: Marginalia — agentic RAG that shows its work

> Status: approved charter (2026-07-08). Name is fixed by the master plan and earns it: *marginalia* are notes written in a text's margins, and the product renders citations literally as live margin notes; the accent comes from *rubrication*, the medieval red-ink annotation practice. Name, UI metaphor, and accent are one idea. Absorbs v1 Marginalia (the RAG concept) and millstone (its document-ingestion role folds into the offline ingest pipeline).

## Problem
Every RAG demo says "trust me"; Marginalia says "check me." RAG systems fail invisibly and the failures are the whole story: (1) **opaque retrieval**, a viewer cannot see why a passage was pulled, so a grounded answer and a confident hallucination look identical; (2) **decorative citations**, most chat-with-docs products render a source list, not claim-to-passage bindings anyone can verify in one click; (3) **unmeasured quality**, almost no public RAG demo publishes its own retrieval metrics, ablations, or regressions. Marginalia applies one product decision three times, at retrieval, at citation, and at the eval tab: never ask for trust you can show. For an AI-infrastructure hiring manager it demonstrates in one screen the exact skills they hire for: hybrid retrieval, reranking, agentic orchestration, eval discipline, and honest instrumentation.

## Evidence
(Every number below was read from a primary source this session; dates inline. Re-verify Cloudflare limits at build start per the master-plan rule.)
- **Hybrid + rerank is measured, not folklore.** Anthropic's contextual-retrieval study: contextual embeddings cut top-20 retrieval failure 35% (5.7% to 3.7%); adding contextual BM25 cut it 49% (to 2.9%); adding reranking cut it 67% (to 1.9%). Source: anthropic.com/news/contextual-retrieval (read 2026-07-08). This justifies the architecture AND becomes a v1 experiment: we replicate the ablation on OUR corpus and publish OUR measured deltas. We never pre-claim Anthropic's numbers as ours.
- **Platform math is real** (developers.cloudflare.com, read 2026-07-08). Vectorize free tier: 5M stored vector dimensions, 30M queried vector dimensions/month, queried-dimension billing = (queries + stored vectors) x dimensions — the stored term is the resident vector count, billed monthly, not one-time inserts (their worked example: (30,000 queries + 10,000 stored) x 768 = 30.72M queried dims, plus 7.68M stored dims), max 1536 dims/vector, topK <=50 with metadata, 10KiB metadata/vector. Workers AI free: 10,000 neurons/day; bge-m3 1,075 neurons/M-tokens (1024d), bge-base-en-v1.5 6,058 n/M (768d), bge-reranker-base 283 n/M, llama-3.3-70b-instruct-fp8-fast 26,668 n/M input + 204,805 n/M output, llama-3.1-8b-instruct 25,608 in + 75,147 out.
- **The corpus is genuinely public domain.** Landmark US federal accident reports (public domain per 17 U.S.C. 105); seed sources located and pinned in docs/corpus-dossier.md (Rogers/Challenger, CAIB/Columbia, Apollo 13 Review Board, Kemeny/Three Mile Island).

## Audience
Primary: AI-infrastructure hiring managers and senior engineers giving the portfolio 90 seconds; one preset question must land a "this person actually understands retrieval" verdict. Secondary: engineers evaluating RAG patterns who arrive from the eval tab or a shared-answer permalink (the eval tab is content marketing aimed exactly at them). Tertiary: agent builders once the MCP surface ships (v1.x).

## Alternatives users have today
NotebookLM (closed, no retrieval trace, no public evals). Perplexity and ChatGPT file uploads (citations link to documents, not audited claim-to-passage bindings; no ablations). LangChain/LlamaIndex demo apps (developer-facing, rarely deployed, never self-evaluating). Cloudflare's own RAG tutorials (ingestion demos, not products). None combines visible hybrid-retrieval provenance, one-click citation audit, and a public honest eval tab on a $0 stack. That combination is the product. ## Success metric (with target and date)
- **Primary (product proof, process-based and un-gameable)**: by **2026-09-08**, the public eval tab is live over a >=60-question golden set on the Postmortem Library with (a) **>=7 consecutive real nightly `eval_run` rows** persisted in D1, (b) the three-way **hybrid vs dense-only vs BM25-only ablation published with OUR measured deltas**, and (c) **LLM-judge-vs-human agreement within 5 points** on the 20-question spot-audit. This metric passes on process and honesty, not on hitting a score — so it cannot be gamed by authoring easy questions or a generous judge, and it does not punish an honest sub-target number. (v0.5 target **2026-08-18**, leaving three weeks of eval runway.) The **hit-rate@5 >=85% and citation-support precision >=90%** figures are *published targets the tab tracks* (misses shown openly, per the product's own thesis), not charter pass/fail gates.
- **Secondary (demo adoption, under the portfolio north star of recruiter/founder sessions reaching a live demo interaction)**: >=30% of **human** demo sessions that receive a completed answer fire `citation_audit_opened`, and >=100 unique human sessions with a grounded answer, within 45 days of public launch (backstop **2026-10-06**). Denominator is defined in the tracking plan as human sessions only (PostHog bot filtering + a minimal-interaction heuristic); a human session whose grounded answer arrives via a preset replay DOES count toward this denominator (the preset is a byte-identical full trace, and on launch day most human traffic lands there — see the launch-risk note in Rollout), while crawler-driven zero-neuron preset replays — which robots.txt explicitly invites — are excluded so the ratio is not diluted by non-human traffic.
- **Guardrails**: p95 TTFT <1s on cached preset questions and <6s on novel questions; zero demo-down days from neuron exhaustion (the degrade ladder engages instead, tracked via `degrade_mode_engaged`); zero eval-tab gaps (a "didn't run" alert on the cron per the automation rules).

## Value metric & monetization shape
Value metric: audited answers over a private corpus (what a paying user maximizes is trustworthy grounded answers on THEIR documents). Marginalia itself is free forever as a portfolio demo. The shape it demonstrates (for this section, not for building) is bring-your-own-corpus with metered ingestion + query credits; AI cost is never flat-rate, per the monetization system. The eval tab is the future pricing page's proof section, exactly as Patchbay's token dashboard is. No pricing surface ships in v1.

## Non-goals (v1)
User-uploaded corpora (BYO-corpus is the v2/commercial shape). Multi-corpus switching. Chat history and multi-turn memory (each question is a fresh, auditable run; a follow-up box may pass prior Q&A as context, but no persistent threads). Accounts or login for the public demo (admin surface only). Fine-tuning or self-hosted models. LLM-generated contextual chunk headers at ingest (a v1 experiment, see docs/eval-spec.md; structural headers ship first). Cloudflare Queues (paid; banned by platform posture). Scanned-page image rendering in the audit panel (v1-optional; text-layer highlighting ships first).

## v0.5 cut (the smallest version that tests the hypothesis)
Hypothesis: visible hybrid retrieval + auditable claim-level citations makes a RAG answer trustworthy in a way a hiring manager can FEEL in 90 seconds. Cut: ingested seed corpus + one question box + 4 to 6 preset questions + **single-pass** hybrid pipeline (no agent loop yet). Query embed, then a parallel FTS5 BM25 lane and a Vectorize dense lane, reciprocal-rank fusion, bge-reranker-base over the fused set, top-k context, one llama-3.3-70b synthesis call emitting inline citation markers, answer rendered with margin-note citations. Full retrieval trace visible (both lanes' candidates, per-lane rank, fused score, rerank score). Clicking a citation opens the audit panel: exact source passage highlighted, document and page anchor, and that chunk's complete retrieval provenance. Preset questions are KV-cached full traces (zero-neuron replays). No eval tab, no agent loop, no share links yet. Success = Sam asks a novel question, gets a grounded cited answer, audits a citation to the exact Rogers Commission passage, with screenshots + queried D1 trace rows as session evidence.

## v1 (the launch shape)
Agent loop: a planner (llama-3.1-8b) decomposes the question into sub-queries, runs retrieval per sub-query, decides sufficiency under an explicit budget (max 4 retrieval steps), then 70b synthesis. Every step streams over SSE as the typed event protocol (status | thinking | tool_call | tool_result | token | error) rendered per the agentic-UI system. Public eval tab: nightly retrieval metrics (hit-rate@1/@5, MRR on all 60 golden questions), weekly LLM-judged faithfulness on a 20-question sentinel subset (sharded 5/night across four nights to fit the combined neuron ceiling), the hybrid/dense/BM25 ablation, and a time-series that keeps regressions visible; powered by promptfoo + custom scorers, results in D1, the tab reads D1 and never runs evals on demand. Share permalinks: every answered question gets a URL that replays the stored trace from D1 (zero neurons to view), the growth loop, since shared answers are self-demonstrating. Citation feedback: "flag this citation" per margin note, surfaced on an admin view, feeding golden-set curation. Degrade ladder as a product feature, not an apology: full agentic, then single-pass, then retrieval-only (hybrid trace + passages, no generation), then cached presets only; a banner states which mode is live and why. Docs + changelog pages, OG cards, robots.txt allowing AI crawlers. v1.x: a remote MCP server surface (search_corpus / ask_corpus / get_passage) so Marginalia registers into Patchbay as a tenant alongside Conduit.

## Activation event
`citation_audit_opened`: a visitor opens a citation's audit panel (source passage + retrieval provenance) after receiving a completed answer. Answer completion only proves the demo ran; opening the audit is the visitor performing the product's thesis (checking the work). It is unambiguous in PostHog (query id + citation marker + ms-since-answer) and sits directly under the north star. TTV target: <60 seconds from landing, via preset questions (one click to a cached, instantly-streaming answer whose margin notes invite the click). Funnel: `demo_session_started` -> `question_asked` (preset|novel) -> `answer_completed` -> `citation_audit_opened` (activation) -> `trace_expanded` / `answer_shared`.

## Rollout
Dogfood on Sam's own questions (v0.5) with screenshots + D1 trace evidence -> public repo + docs + corpus-dossier page -> nightly eval cron accumulates >=7 consecutive real runs -> Show HN: "RAG that grades itself in public," the eval tab as the story -> feature re-launches per changelog -> v1.x MCP surface registers Marginalia as Patchbay's second tenant.

**Launch-day novel-question capacity is an explicit risk.** Inside the 4,000/day combined ceiling, the demo sustains only ~10 typical (~8 worst-case) novel agentic questions on a non-shard night and ~4 on the four faithfulness-shard nights before the degrade ladder engages (ARCHITECTURE "Free-allocation math"). A Show HN spike or a batch of same-day recruiters means most novel questions land in retrieval-only mode, not full agentic synthesis. The launch experience is therefore designed to lean on the preset questions (unlimited zero-neuron byte-identical full-trace replays — the "feel it in 90 seconds" moment holds via presets) plus retrieval-only for overflow, with the degrade banner as the honest surface stating which mode is live and why. Novel-question wow is a bonus on a quiet hour, not the load-bearing launch path; the eval tab (the Show HN story) is entirely neuron-free to view.
